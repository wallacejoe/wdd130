Overview

Purpose

The primary purposes of my website are to attract and educate clients. To accomplish these goals, I will show potential clients what we have to offer as far as location and adventures. We will also offer a week-long training course to help begginers feel confident on the water. My goal is for this to be a fun, safe place for customers to come and enjoy experiencing what nature has to offer. This site should help customers determine which adventures they are best suited for and describe the precautions we have in place to ensure the safety of our guests.

Audience

The expected audience for my site are those looking to enjoy and experience nature in a fun and exciting way. Our audience can consist of anyone older than 10 who pass a health screening. Fitness tests will be required of anyone wanting the more difficult adventures. Minors must be accompanied by an adult.
